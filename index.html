Rainbow Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Food coloring (red, blue, yellow)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk and egg until smooth.
  3. Divide batter into three bowls and add food coloring to each.
  4. Cook pancakes on a greased skillet over medium heat.
  5. Stack and serve with syrup or fruit!
