Yogurt Parfait

Layered goodness from Breakfast Bays, kid-approved!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed fruit (e.g., berries, banana slices)

Instructions

  1. In a clear glass or bowl, add a layer of yogurt (about 1/3 cup).
  2. Sprinkle a layer of granola (about 2 tbsp) over the yogurt.
  3. Add a layer of mixed fruit (about 2 tbsp).
  4. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Serve immediately or chill for later!
