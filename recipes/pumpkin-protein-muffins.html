Pumpkin Protein Muffins

Nutritious, soft muffins from Breakfast Bays, great for breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup protein powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix pumpkin puree, flour, and protein powder in a bowl.
  3. Pour into a greased muffin tin.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool and serve.
