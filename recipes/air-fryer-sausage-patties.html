Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground sausage (mild or spicy)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp maple syrup for sweetness

Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix sausage with garlic powder, onion powder, pepper, and maple syrup (if using).
  3. Form into small, flat patties.
  4. Place patties in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Don’t overcrowd.
  5. Air fry 8–10 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and cooked through.
  6. Let cool slightly and enjoy with your favorite breakfast!
