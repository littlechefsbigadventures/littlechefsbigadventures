Air Fryer Sausage Patties: A Picky Eater’s Breakfast Win

Quick, crispy, and kid-approved!

Serves: 4
Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground sausage (mild)
  • 1 tsp paprika or garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix sausage and spices until well combined.
  2. Form into 2‑inch patties (or fun shapes with cookie cutters!).
  3. Air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes, flipping halfway.
  4. Serve warm with toast, eggs, or fruit.

Why This Works

Golden-crispy texture delightful for tiny hands. Ready fast with minimal cleanup—perfect for breakfast adventures!

Chef’s Tips

  • Let kids help shape patties—use cutters for stars or hearts!
  • Offer dipping sauces: ketchup, mustard, or mild sauce make it fun.
  • Make mini patties for smaller mouths and big confidence boosts.
  • Celebrate the crunch: “Hear that crunchy sound? All done!”
