Mini Pancake Bites

Tiny pancakes from Breakfast Bays, fun to dip!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, milk, and sugar until smooth.
  3. Pour batter into a greased mini muffin tin, filling each cup halfway.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes until golden and puffed.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or fruit for dipping!
